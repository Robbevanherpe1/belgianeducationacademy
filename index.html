BELGIAN EDUCATION ACADEMY

We are a course provider in Belgium, near Ghent and Brussels, we offer Erasmus+ courses for teachers and staff.

Providing Courses

We provide courses for teachers by teachers,to explore the belgian educational system.

City Trip 

It is possible to book an extra day to visit Antwerp or Brussels.

KA1 Funding 

You can apply for KA1 funding from your national agency. This fund covers travel costs, accommodation, and the course fee (€440).
